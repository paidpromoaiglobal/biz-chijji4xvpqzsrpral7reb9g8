About Us

RC Fitness Gym

RC Fitness Gym is Mallur's premier fitness establishment, proudly located on Trichy Main Road opposite Modern High School in Salem, Tamil Nadu. With an outstanding rating of 4.9 out of 5, we have built a reputation for delivering real results through dedicated coaching, a motivating environment, and state-of-the-art workout facilities. Whether you are a beginner taking your first step toward a healthier lifestyle or an experienced athlete looking to push your limits, RC Fitness Gym is the place for you.

Our doors open early in the morning and stay open through the evening, six days a week, with special Sunday morning sessions — because we believe your fitness journey should never be interrupted by a busy schedule. Our passionate trainers are committed to guiding every member with personalized attention, proper technique, and consistent motivation. Join our growing family of fitness enthusiasts and experience the RC Fitness Gym difference today.
